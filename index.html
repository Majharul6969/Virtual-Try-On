What is Urolithin A and why is it important?

Urolithin A is a next-generation cellular nutrient that supports mitophagy — your body's natural process of recycling damaged mitochondria and replacing them with healthy new ones. Think of it as a cellular "deep clean." As we age, this process slows down, leading to less energy, slower recovery, and accelerated aging. Clinical research has shown Urolithin A can help restore this process, supporting improved muscle endurance, cellular energy, and healthy aging.
Fun fact: Only about 40% of people naturally produce enough Urolithin A from food alone. Supplementation helps ensure your cells get what they need regardless of your gut microbiome.
